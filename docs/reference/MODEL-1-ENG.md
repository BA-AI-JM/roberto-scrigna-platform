## 1. Meal-exchange system (the item-10 core)

The model defines meal-slot-specific FOOD CLASSES. A substitution is valid only
within the same class AND the same meal slot, at the gram quantity of the matching
tier. Tiers scale by protein/carb/fat content, not 1:1 grams.

### 1.1 COLAZIONE (breakfast)

- Beverage: tea (2 bags) or coffee — 1 cup; skimmed milk / kefir / soy milk 250 mL.
- **Protein class (tiers ~20g → ~40g protein):**
  Greek yogurt 0% / Skyr · LIDL-MILK-Muller protein dessert · low-fat natural or soy
  yogurt (+whey top-up) · lean white kefir (+whey) · skimmed or vegetable milk (+whey) ·
  cottage cheese / light ricotta / quark · egg whites (albumen) / Yoeggs (omelets or
  pancakes) · protein shake ("quicker choice") · lean sliced cured meat (e.g. chicken
  ham) · whole eggs (± egg whites; nut adjustment in-meal) · protein bar.
  Tier examples: 20g-pro tier = 200g greek yogurt | 150g yogurt+15g whey | 3 whole
  eggs (−4 nuts) | 80–100g cured meat; 30g tier = 300g yogurt | 120g cured meat |
  2 whole+200mL whites; 40g tier = 400g yogurt | 200g cured meat | 3 whole+300mL whites.
- **Fat class (tiers ~4g → ~60g fat):** walnuts/almonds · peanut/pistachio/hazelnut
  butter · dried nuts (pistachio, cashew, hazelnut…) · avocado (pulp grams) · dark
  chocolate ≥80% · fresh coconut. Tier anchors: 1 walnut/4 almonds ≈ 4g → 10 walnuts/
  40 almonds ≈ 60g; chocolate 5g → 40g; avocado 10g → 100g pulp.
- **Fruit class:** seasonal or dehydrated — ½ fruit / ~75g / 15g dehydrated (9–12g CHO);
  1 fruit / ~150g / 30g dehydrated (18–23g CHO); 2 fruits / ~300g / 60g dehydrated.
- **Carb class (tiers ~20g → ~65g+ CHO):** rice/corn/wasa cakes or rusks (8g each) ·
  cereal/rye/wholemeal bread · wholegrain crackers · oats/muesli/cornflakes/All-Bran
  (incl. Coco Pops) · fruit as add-on. Tier anchors: 3 cakes | 40g bread | 20g oats
  (≈20g CHO) → 16 cakes | 200g bread | 130g oats (≈120g+); sweet add-ons: honey/jam
  2 tsp (~20g) → 6 tsp (~60g); fruit juice 130–250 mL.

### 1.2 SPUNTINO (snack)

Protein-centric class (tiers ~20g → ~35g protein): Greek yogurt 0% / Skyr · low-fat
plain / soy yogurt / plain kefir (+whey) · protein dessert (LIDL/MILK) · Skyrella /
Granarolo Hi-Pro mozzarella · protein shake (fastest option) · lean cold cuts
(chicken, turkey, roast beef) · rinsed lupins / plant-based cold cuts · fresh 0% fat
cheeses (cottage, quark) · egg whites / Yoeggs · protein bar (~25g). Meal-fat
reduction notes apply to lupins/cheese tiers.

### 1.3 PRANZO / CENA — PROTEIN class (tiers ~17g → ~70g protein)

White meat / loin / lean fish · red meat / fatty fish (−oil in meal) · whole eggs
(+ whites; −oil) · natural canned tuna / mackerel · lean cured meat · 0% fat cheeses
(quark/cottage; −oil) · tempeh / soy burger / tofu / edamame (−oil) · seitan / Quorn
(fillets). Tier anchors: 80g meat / 120g fish ≈ 17–22g pro → 300g meat / 400g fish ≈
60–70g pro; eggs scale 3 whole+2 whites → 4 whole+350mL whites; tofu-class 100g → 400g.

### 1.4 PRANZO / CENA — CARBO class (tiers ~20g → ~235g CHO)

Whole grain cereals / rice / pasta (int.) · potatoes / sweet potatoes · wholemeal or
cereal bread · legumes / legume pasta (50g dry ≈ 250g canned) · rice/corn cakes / wasa.
Refined variants (white rice/pasta/bread) appear from the ~70–80g-CHO tiers upward.
Anchors: 30g cereals | 100g potatoes | 40g bread (≈20–25g CHO) → 300g cereals | 1kg
potatoes | 450g bread | 40 cakes (≈215–235g CHO). Legumes stop scaling ~100g dry.

### 1.5 VERDURE (vegetables)

- Seasonal: small plate 100–200g (≈8–15g CHO) · medium dish 150–250g (10–15g) ·
  large ~300g. Leafy vegetables → may add 10–15g cereal-carbs to the meal.
- Raw + cooked combo: two medium dishes + 30–60g leafy.
- Fibrous class: artichokes, Brussels sprouts, chicory, cabbage, broccoli, fennel,
  asparagus… Non-fibrous/soft class: tomatoes, cucumbers, courgettes, carrots,
  peppers, peeled aubergine, leafy/creamy-cooked.
- High-nitrate (performance): HIGH beetroot, spinach, rocket, basil, celery,
  watercress, chervil, lettuce (200g) · MED-HIGH celeriac, Chinese cabbage, endive,
  fennel, kohlrabi, leek, parsley (200–300g) · MED cabbage, dill, turnip, savoy (300g).

### 1.6 GRASSI (meal seasoning; tiers ~5g → ~40g fat, ~120 kcal steps)

One choice or proportional mix: olive oil EVO (1 tsp 12g → 3 tbsp) · Genoese pesto ·
butter 7–9g → 33g · Grana/Parmesan 10–12g → 90g (or 25g slices) · dark chocolate
80–90% 10g → 60g · dried nuts 15g → 55g · avocado 25g → 150g · mayonnaise 30 mL tier.

### 1.7 Peri-workout

PRE: coffee or green tea (2 bags) or pre-workout stack. INTRA: water.
POST: water = 120–150% of weight lost in training (weigh before/after), net of
liquids consumed.

## 2. Supplements

**Base:** creatine monohydrate 3–6g/day with a meal (post-WO on training days;
Creapure) · whey protein isolate · vitamin D 2000–4000 UI (or 20' sun) · beta-alanine
6g with meal (paresthesia — split 3×2g or 2×3g) · omega-3 high EPA+DHA IFOS 4 pearls
with meal · slow-release vitamin C (C-Tard) 500mg morning · complete multivitamin 1/day.

**When needed:** magnesium L-threonate/bisglycinate 200–400mg 30–60' pre-bed ·
L-theanine 200–400mg 45' pre-WO or pre-bed · melatonin 0.5→5mg titrate · zinc citrate
~150mg (≈50mg zinc) · iron bisglycinate (Ferrodyn) 1 tab AM + 1g vit C (no coffee/tea/
fiber/calcium in that meal) · glucosamine 2×1g main meals · chondroitin 1g with meal ·
boswellia serrata 2.4–3.6g in 2–3 doses with meals · myo-inositol 4–6g pre-breakfast.

**Pre-workout (as needed):** caffeine 100–300mg 45'–1h pre (titrate; caution evening) ·
L-theanine 200–400mg · Bulk Pump stim-free 26g 45'–1h pre cardio-metabolic (has 3g
creatine) or Myprotein Origin.

**Intestinal rebalancing:** glutamine 2×5g (AM+PM) · probiotics VSL#3 1 sachet/day
×10 days (severe: 2/day ×5) · prebiotics inulin/psyllium 5–10g evening.

**Adaptogens:** rhodiola 250–600mg · ashwagandha 300–500mg with meal · panax ginseng
400mg. **Insulin mimetics:** Ceylon cinnamon 1–6g · berberine 1–2g pre-meals · ALA
600mg ×2.

## 3. Fight-week / weight-cut protocols

### 3.1 Acute weight manipulation
- Lose 1–2% BW: water loading + salt removal + fiber reduction.
- Up to 4–5% BW: add 3–6 days carb restriction, calories replaced by fat.
- Creatine OFF 7 days pre-weigh-in if >6% above class limit.
- Fight week: everything freshly cooked; NO deli meats, canned tuna, bread, salted
  rice cakes, preserved foods, added-salt foods (incl. cheeses, butter); labels must
  not list salt.

### 3.2 Day-by-day template (example table)
Day −7: water 3.5L, salt 2.5g — cut carbs 30–50% (by weight/training).
−6: 3.5L / 2.5g — 2100 kcal, P178 F105 C116 (meal templates: 5 eggs+oil+fruit+bread;
meat/fish+veg+EVO; yog+choc+fruit; meat/fish+veg+EVO).
−5: 6L / 1g — 2100, P178 F120 C85 (bread→avocado).
−4: 8.5L / 0.5g — same + pre-training 20–30g honey.
−3, −2: 8.5L / 0g — 2100, P178 F120 C62 (veg removed, avocado up).
−1: 0.5–1L / 0g — no dinner.
WEIGH-IN: 0L, fasted.
POST (INS): deficit-to-class ÷ 3 → 2/3 liquids (rehydration solution) + 1/3
calorically dense food (e.g. 50g protein bar + 100g rice cakes + 50g jam).

### 3.3 Day before / day of weigh-in
Glass of water (100–200mL) saved for night; chewing gum for thirst; optional natural
diuretic (pilosella/dandelion) + intestinal-regularity teas. If weight already low
after one dehydration round: optional light evening item (chamomile, frozen yogurt,
small ice cream; ≤100–250g) — "always text me first".
Cutting work: 15–30' cycles (active: light movement/sauna/hot bath; passive: blankets
+ towels), repeat to target, monitoring weight/sweat/alertness/vitals.

### 3.4 Rehydration + refeed
0–1h post weigh-in: 1.5–2L REIDRAX-class solution (500mL immediately, 250mL at
5–10', then 250mL every 15–20').
First meal: mostly liquids + easily digestible carbs (rice cream, pudding, banana
bread, puffed cereals, crispbread, honey/jam, fruit purée). Then non-wholegrain carbs
+ moderate simple carbs, optional lean protein (100g chicken/turkey/bresaola or whey);
NO vegetables. End each meal: creatine 4–6g + digestive enzymes (Metadigest 1–2 tabs).
1–2h: 20g simple + 50g complex carbs + 20–30g protein + REIDRAX + 6g creatine + enzymes.
Macros: 20–35g pro, 1–2 g/kg carbs (70–160g), minimal fat/fiber.
2–3h: 130g complex carbs + 30g lean protein + 5–13g fat + soft veg (small) + REIDRAX/
coconut water + 6g creatine + enzymes.
3h+: carbs 1.2 g/kg/h + ~20g lean protein/meal + creatine + enzymes + BIOCHETASI +
VSL#3. 4–6 meals/day; if stomach closed → liquid calories (shakes, rice cream,
semolina). End of each meal: creatine + enzymes + ~½ tsp salt "shot".
Fluids: ≥1L/h for first 4–6h (250mL/15').
Creatine total 20–30g/day on weigh-in + fight day. Salt: use plenty.

### 3.5 Fight day
Meals by match time (15–18: breakfast+lunch+small liquid snack; 18–23: +snack,
possibly dinner). Approaching the fight: shrink portions, drop protein/fiber, go
liquid. Templates: breakfast plant milk+whey+Cheerios+juice; lunch white pasta/rice
+bresaola+beets/spinach+oil+1 Coca-Cola; early-match snack rice cakes+jam+juice;
late-match snack white bread+turkey+juice+Haribo; optional dinner white rice+parmesan.
60' pre: caffeine 300–400mg + theanine 400mg + citrulline 6g or Beet-It; ~300mL fluids.
5' pre: 1 tsp honey (10–20g) or ginger candies.
Fight-week supplementation: multivitamin ×2, C-Tard 3g (1+1+1), omega-3 ×4, vitamin D
Dibase 10.000 IU/mL 5–10 drops, probiotics (Probactiol Duo / VSL3) from day −7;
optional glutamine 2×5g, zinc ×2 AM, NAC 600mg ×2.
Water/salt rules: big boluses (250mL/20' or 300–400mL/30'), never too much too fast;
salt grams = daily total, 0 means none; training days: drink 150% of sweat loss on
top. Cramps: TRPA1 spicy foods (chili, ginger, wasabi…); during cramp: acetic acid
(pickle brine, mustard packet). Fight-week veg (<10g fiber/day): asparagus tips,
spinach/cavolo nero leaves, cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, skinned zucchini, peeled
pepper, pumpkin, blueberries/strawberries, avocado, aromatic herbs, ginger/lemon
zest, no-salt passata.

### 3.6 Cold exposure protocol
Goals: inflammation ↓, mood, recovery, immunity. 10–15°C → 10–15'; <10°C → 3–10'.
Cold showers vs immersion. Frequency: min 1×/wk, optimal 2–3×; mornings best;
keep distant from strength/hypertrophy sessions, closer to endurance/recovery.
Progression from 1–2' at 15–18°C. Precautions: hypertension/cardio conditions;
supervise early sessions; ≤10' below 10°C without experience. Rewarm gradually;
Wim Hof breathing optional (advanced).

### 3.7 Heat acclimation
Start ≥4 weeks pre-cut (3–5 wks depending on frequency). Passive: hot bath 38.5–42°C
(15' → 60' total in reps) or sauna 70–100°C (≤15–20' cycles + 5' rest). Frequency:
min 2×/wk, ideal 3–4×; late afternoon/evening. Monitor: weight pre/post → sweat rate
(kg/h), session times, symptoms. Risks: electrolyte imbalance, dehydration, heat
stroke. Homemade electrolyte: 1L water + 1.5g salt + juice of 1 lemon. SIS GO HYDRO
tabs recommended. Stop on dizziness/confusion/irregular heartbeat.

### 3.8 Cutting kit (pharmacy + equipment)
Metadigest Total (enzymes) · Maalox/Gaviscon · REIDRAX/Idravita/Pedialyte/Liquid IV
(2–4L solution) · Imodium (emergency) · Domperidone/Geffer (emergency) · probiotics
VSL#3/Probactiol Duo · Esoxx ONE (antireflux). Equipment: Sweet Sweat cream, thermal
blanket, portable sauna, ginger candies, heavy clothing, finger pulse oximeter, blood
pressure monitor, bath thermometer (Amazon links in source PDF).
